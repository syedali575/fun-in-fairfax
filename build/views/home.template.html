This website is dedicated to residents and visitors of Fairfax County, who intend to plan a day of indoor and/or outdoor fun activities. This website allows you to look up parks, libraries, reccreational center and shopping centers in your vicinity.

Fairfax County offers a beautiful collection of parks and out door activities to its residents and visitors. Visitors can enjoy many activities such as hiking , bicycling, picnic, camping, boating, fishing, train rides, kayaking, etc. Fairfax County facilities are well maintained and hence very well utilized by its residents.
