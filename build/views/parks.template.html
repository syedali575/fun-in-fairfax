Parks

Fairfax County has a number of parks—from big to small—that offer a range of opportunities for hours of fun. They include historic buildings, gorgeous lakes, scenic vegetation, and lots of opportunity to commune with nature Come row or pedal a boat, engage in a friendly game of disc golf, let the kids run around a playground, take a ride on a carousel, hike or bike a nature trail, mini-golf, take a train ride, play basketball, kick around a soccer ball, barbecue some food, or just spread out a blanket and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
