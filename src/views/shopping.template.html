Shopping Centers

Fairfax Shopping Centers offer a wide variety of options such as stores, theaters, restuarants, coffee shops. There is practically something to do for everyone. Fuel up with caffeine and a bite to eat at any number of local cafes and coffeeshops. While some are best for a quick drop in on your way to a meet up, others offer comfortable seating, Wifi, and a great ambience for catching up with friends. Fairfax is known for having a large number—and great variety—of eateries. Prices vary and many are family-friendly. There’s something for even the pickiest of eaters. Fairfax’s ethnic restaurants, in particular, are not to be missed!
